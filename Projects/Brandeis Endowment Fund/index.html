Project Overview

This project involves the strategic management and optimization of the Brandeis University Endowment Fund, leveraging Excel and VBA to develop a diversified portfolio that aligns with the university’s risk and return objectives. We utilized capital market expectations, selected specific asset classes, and incorporated the Black-Litterman model to refine portfolio allocations based on both historical data and analyst opinions.

Project Details

  • Client: Brandeis University Endowment Fund
  • Objective: Optimize investment returns to support 15% of the university’s operating budget, with a focus on diversification and risk management.
  • Technologies Used: Excel, VBA for data analysis and portfolio optimization, Black-Litterman model
  • Date: October 2024
